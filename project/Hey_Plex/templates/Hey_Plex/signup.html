Our Rental Terms

BOOKING


A client can book more than one vehicle before the pickup date.After the pickup date he/she must return the lended vehicle before booking another one.

DRIVING LICENSE


A client must have a valid driving license.He/She must be over 21 years old. The client must also have a identification card....

DEPOSIT


A client must deposit 50% of the amount of money needed to hire a vehicle for the specified number of days. The remaining amount will be paid when acquiring the vehicle in our offices. All payments are done through MPesa.

FUEL


Renting costs do not include fuel

INSURANCE


All vehicles are insured but any irresponsible damage e.g. loss of vehicle properties will be catered by the client. The client must take care of the vehicle to avoid theft.If the vehicle is stolen, He/She will cater for all the expenses required to track the vehicle
